Beef Bourguignon

🥩
⏱ 3–4 hours 👥 Serves 6 📅 Weekend

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg beef chuck, cut into 5cm cubes
  • 200g lardons (or thick-cut bacon)
  • 1 bottle red Burgundy (or any full-bodied red)
  • 300g pearl onions
  • 300g cremini mushrooms
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • Thyme, bay leaves, beef stock

Steps

  1. Pat beef dry. Brown in batches in a hot Dutch oven — don't crowd the pan.
  2. Sauté lardons, set aside. Cook onions and garlic in the same pot.
  3. Return beef, add wine, stock, tomato paste, herbs. Liquid should just cover.
  4. Cover and braise in oven at 160°C for 2.5–3 hours until fork-tender.
  5. Sauté mushrooms separately, add in the last 30 minutes.
  6. Taste, adjust seasoning. Rest 15 min before serving.
Julia's note: Don't rush the browning step. That's where the flavor lives. And use a wine you'd actually drink.

Serve With

  • Buttered egg noodles or mashed potato
  • Crusty bread to mop up the sauce
  • Same wine you cooked with
