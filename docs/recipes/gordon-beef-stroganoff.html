Beef Stroganoff

⏱ 30 min 👥 Serves 4
Watch Gordon Ramsay make it on YouTube

Steps

  1. Sear beef strips in very hot pan in batches. Remove.
  2. Cook shallots and mushrooms in butter until golden.
  3. Add paprika, mustard, stock. Simmer 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in sour cream on low heat. Return beef.
  5. Heat through gently — don't boil or cream will split.
Gordon's tip: Cook the beef in small batches — overcrowding the pan makes it steam instead of sear.

Serve With

  • Buttered egg noodles
  • Chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • A dollop of extra sour cream
