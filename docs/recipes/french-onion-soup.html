French Onion Soup

⏱ 1.5 hrs 👥 Serves 4
Watch Julia Child make it
Search on YouTube →

Steps

  1. Slice onions thinly. Cook in butter and oil over low heat 45 minutes, stirring often, until deep golden.
  2. Add wine, cook 5 minutes. Add stock, simmer 20 minutes. Season.
  3. Ladle soup into oven-safe bowls. Top with baguette slices.
  4. Cover generously with grated Gruyère. Broil until bubbly and golden.
Julia's tip: Don't rush the onions. 45 minutes minimum. This is where all the flavor comes from.

Serve With

  • As a starter or a light meal on its own
  • Extra baguette on the side
