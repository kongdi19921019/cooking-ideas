The French Omelette

⏱ 5 min 👥 Serves 1
Watch Julia Child make it on YouTube

Steps

  1. Break eggs into bowl, season, beat just until yolks and whites are combined.
  2. Heat butter in small pan over high heat until foaming subsides.
  3. Pour in eggs. Immediately shake pan while stirring with a fork.
  4. When eggs are just barely set, tilt pan and roll omelette onto plate.
  5. It should be pale yellow outside, creamy inside.
Julia's tip: The whole thing takes 30 seconds once the butter is ready. High heat, fast hands, don't overthink it.

Serve With

  • Simple green salad
  • A glass of white wine
  • Crusty baguette

Ingredients

eggs
Base recipe: 3 eggs · Serves 1
