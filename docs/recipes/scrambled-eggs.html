Perfect Scrambled Eggs

⏱ 10 min 👥 Serves 2 ⚡ Quick
Watch Gordon Ramsay make it
Search on YouTube →

Steps

  1. Crack eggs straight into a cold non-stick pan. Add butter. No pre-whisking.
  2. Medium heat. Stir constantly with a rubber spatula — keep it moving.
  3. Every 30 seconds, take the pan off the heat for 10 seconds. Keep stirring.
  4. When eggs are softly set but still slightly wet, take off heat completely.
  5. Stir in crème fraîche. Season. Plate immediately.
Gordon's rule: Never stop stirring. Never walk away. The moment you think it needs more time, it's already overcooked.

Serve With

  • Toasted sourdough
  • Smoked salmon on the side
  • A good coffee

Ingredients

eggs
