Tarte Tatin

⏱ 1 hr 👥 Serves 8
Watch Julia Child make it
Search on YouTube →

Steps

  1. Make caramel in an oven-safe pan: melt sugar until amber. Add butter.
  2. Peel, halve and core apples. Arrange tightly in caramel, cut-side up.
  3. Cook on stovetop 10 minutes until apples start to soften.
  4. Drape pastry over apples, tucking edges down.
  5. Bake at 200°C for 25-30 minutes until pastry is golden.
  6. Cool 5 minutes, then flip onto a serving plate.
Julia's tip: Let the tart rest exactly 5 minutes before flipping — too soon and it falls apart, too late and the caramel sticks.

Serve With

  • Crème fraîche or vanilla ice cream
  • Serve warm
