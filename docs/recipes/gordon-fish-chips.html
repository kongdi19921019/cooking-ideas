Fish & Chips

⏱ 1 hr 👥 Serves 4
Watch Gordon Ramsay make it
Search on YouTube →

Steps

  1. Chips: par-boil potatoes 10 min, drain, steam dry. Fry at 130°C until cooked through. Drain. Fry again at 180°C until golden.
  2. Make batter: whisk flour, baking powder, beer and pinch of salt.
  3. Dust fish in flour, dip in batter, fry at 180°C for 5-6 minutes until golden.
  4. Drain on paper towel, season immediately.
Gordon's tip: Triple-cooking the chips (boil, fry at low temp, fry at high temp) is the secret to fluffy insides and crispy outsides.

Serve With

  • Mushy peas
  • Tartare sauce
  • Malt vinegar
